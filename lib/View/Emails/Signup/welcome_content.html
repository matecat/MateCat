Getting Started with Matecat

  • Open Matecat and log in to your account.
  • Select the source and target languages.
  • Click the "More settings" button to adjust the project settings according to your needs. You can create translation memories and use them for your projects, choose a machine translation engine and adjust other advanced settings.
  • Upload the files to translate (drag and drop them or browse your computer and select them). Matecat supports a wide range of file formats including .docx, .xlsx, .pdf, and many more.
  • Click "Analyze" to start the project's creation.
  • Once the analysis is complete, click "Translate" to open the editor and start translating your document. Use the translation memory and machine translation matches to translate faster and maintain consistency.
  • Confirm each translated segment by clicking the "Translated" button or pressing ctrl/cmd + enter. At the bottom of the page, you'll be able to track your progress.
  • Once you're done, download the translation by clicking the download button on the top bar.
Start translating!
